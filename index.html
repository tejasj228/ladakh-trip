Day by Day Itinerary 7 days across the Himalayan high desert

01
Arrival

Arrival at Leh

Acclimatization Day

Leh, 3,524 m
Pickup from Leh Airport & transfer to NHPC Guest House
Morning & afternoon rest for acclimatization at altitude
Evening visit to the Hall of Fame Museum
Attend the patriotic Light and Sound Show
Overnight — Leh
02
Nubra

Leh to Nubra Valley

Via Khardung La Pass

Khardung La, 5,359 m
Drive over Khardung La Pass — one of the world's highest motorable roads at 5,359 m
Visit Diskit Monastery — an ancient gompa overlooking the Nubra valley
Explore Hunder Sand Dunes — a surreal cold desert landscape with double-humped camels
Overnight — Nubra Valley
03
Pangong

Nubra to Pangong Lake

Via Shyok River Route

Pangong Tso, 4,350 m
Scenic drive along the Shyok River route through dramatic gorges
Arrive at Pangong Tso — the iconic high-altitude saltwater lake stretching into Tibet
Witness the mesmerising shifting shades of blue and turquoise across the lake
Overnight — Pangong Lake Camp
04
Return

Pangong to Leh

Via Chang La Pass

Chang La, 5,360 m
Return to Leh via Chang La Pass at 5,360 m
Visit Thiksey Monastery — a stunning 12-storey complex resembling the Potala Palace
Explore Shey Palace — the former summer capital of Ladakh's royal family
Overnight — Leh
05
Kargil

Leh to Kargil

Srinagar Highway

Kargil, 2,676 m
Stop at Magnetic Hill — the famous optical illusion where vehicles appear to roll uphill
Visit Pathar Sahib Gurudwara — a sacred Sikh shrine at 3,000 m
Witness Sangam — the dramatic confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers
Explore Lamayuru Monastery — perched dramatically on a moonscape plateau
Local roaming and exploration in Kargil town
Overnight — Kargil
06
Leh

Kargil to Leh

Return Journey

Free Evening
Return drive to Leh
Evening free for Leh Market — pashmina, thangka art, Tibetan jewellery
Overnight — Leh
07
Farewell

Departure

Until Next Time, Ladakh

Leh Airport
Transfer to Leh Airport for your onward journey — carry the mountains with you
